# 🌱 CROP-X

**Smart Crop Advisory System for Small and Marginal Farmers**

Built for **Smart India Hackathon 2025** — Problem Statement ID `SIH25010`, Theme: *Agricultural, FoodTech & Rural Development* (Team CROP-X, Team ID `MITADTSW016`).

> "Turning crop repetition into smart crop choices for better market returns."

## 🎯 About

CROP-X is a one-stop digital platform combining crop advisory, a marketplace, and a farmer community into a single mobile-friendly app — built by a team for small and marginal farmers, available in **English, Marathi, and Punjabi**.

The problem: farmers often repeat the same crops season after season without data-backed guidance, leading to poor market returns, avoidable input costs, and unmanaged risk. CROP-X addresses this by combining advisory tools, cost-saving marketplace access, and community support in one place.

## ✅ Full Feature Set

- 🧪 Locate Soil Testing Labs — find nearby government soil testing facilities
- 🌾 Smart Crop Suggestions — recommends crops based on soil test reports
- ⛅ 14-Day Weather Forecast — forecasts with optimal days for spraying/harvesting
- 🧴 Pesticides & Fertilizers — access agri-inputs online at lower prices
- 📜 Govt. Schemes & Crop Insurance — guidance on relevant schemes and insurance
- 👷 Find a Helper — hire field helpers for day-to-day farm tasks
- 👥 Farmers' Community — connect and share knowledge with other farmers
- 🚜 Machinery & Tools for Rent — access equipment without heavy upfront investment
- 🤝 Contract Farming Guidance — step-by-step support for profitable partnerships
- 📈 Market Price Updates — real-time crop market price tracking
